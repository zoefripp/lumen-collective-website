Finding the right NDIS Support Coordinator in Newcastle or the Illawarra means finding someone who knows the local area, understands the regional provider landscape, and has the time to genuinely invest in your situation.

Here's what to look for — and how Lumen Collective supports participants in these regions.

## NDIS Support Coordination in the Illawarra

The Illawarra region — including Wollongong, Shellharbour, Kiama and surrounding areas — has a growing NDIS provider landscape, but participants can still face challenges finding coordinators who know the area well and have genuine availability.

A good Illawarra Support Coordinator will:

- Know the local provider landscape — who the reliable allied health practitioners are, which community services have capacity, and how local hospitals and health services operate
- Be available in person when that matters — for complex care meetings, hospital liaison, or key planning conversations
- Maintain low caseloads so they can give your situation the attention it needs

## NDIS Support Coordination in Newcastle

Newcastle and the Hunter region have a strong community services sector, but navigating it as an NDIS participant — especially with complex needs — still requires a coordinator who understands the local landscape.

Whether you're coordinating supports across multiple providers, navigating the intersection of the NDIS and the mental health system, or supporting a young person building independence, local knowledge makes a real difference.
