## What Does a Support Coordinator Do?

A Support Coordinator helps you make the most of your NDIS plan. This includes:

- **Understanding your plan** — explaining your funding categories and what they can be used for
- **Finding providers** — connecting you with therapists, support workers, and services in Wollongong and the Illawarra
- **Coordinating your supports** — making sure everything is working together and your providers are communicating
- **Problem solving** — helping you navigate challenges, resolve issues with providers, and prepare for plan reviews
- **Building your capacity** — supporting you to develop skills and confidence to manage your own supports over time
